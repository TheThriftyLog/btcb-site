A Bookshop With Soul

Nestled inside the Shops at Mud Queen — a quaint farmhouse building shared with fellow independent small businesses — Between the Covers is a place where the love of books is palpable. We believe that every great love story begins with the perfect book.

Our shelves are thoughtfully curated with everything from heartwarming novels to magical adventures, new releases to beloved used editions, representing voices and stories from every corner of the world. Whether you wander in for a specific title or just to get lost between the shelves, you'll find something here that speaks to you.

— Logan & the BTCB crew

What Awaits You

More than a store — a destination for readers

Shop Online

Browse our carefully curated collection from the comfort of your reading nook. We ship directly from publishers on most titles — bringing the bookshop to your doorstep without the wait.

Visit In Person

There's nothing quite like running your fingers along real spines. Come get lost in our shelves, chat with our booksellers, and discover something you didn't know you needed.

Special Orders

Can't find what you're looking for? We'll track it down. From rare editions to the latest releases, if it's in print, we can get it for you — often at publisher-direct speed.
